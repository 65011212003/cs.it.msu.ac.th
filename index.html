Computer Science

Mahasarakham University

Welcome to our vibrant and dynamic community of students, academic staff, and researchers dedicated to advancing the field of computing. Our department combines strong theoretical foundations with practical applications, offering comprehensive courses and research opportunities that prepare students for today's rapidly evolving tech landscape. Our distinguished faculty brings extensive real-world experience and expertise to create an enriching learning environment.

Academic Excellence

We are committed to making computer science accessible and engaging for all students. From foundational programming courses to advanced specialized topics, our curriculum provides a solid technical foundation while fostering innovation and creativity. Our thriving research culture spans cutting-edge areas including artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer systems, and network security.

Our faculty regularly secures grants from government agencies, foundations, and industry partners, creating opportunities for students to engage in impactful research. Whether your goal is a career in academia, industry, or government, we provide the skills, knowledge, and hands-on experience needed for success.

Our undergraduate program prepares students for immediate entry into industry positions or advancement to graduate studies. Both paths offer excellent career prospects and opportunities for growth.

For those seeking advanced education, our MSc program offers specialized tracks and research opportunities in emerging technologies. PhD candidates work alongside leading researchers to develop innovative solutions to complex challenges impacting science, technology, and society.

Undergraduate Program

Computer Science & Technology

Our program provides comprehensive training in computer science fundamentals and emerging technologies. Students develop practical skills and innovative thinking through mentor-led research and hands-on projects.

Program Options

Single Degree

Four-year B.Sc. in Computer Science and Technology from MSU.

Dual Degree

A collaboration with Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunication (CQUPT). Students receive:

  • BSc Computer Science and Technology from MSU
  • BEng Computer Science and Technology from CQUPT

Students study 2 years at MSU followed by 2 years at CQUPT.
